## **1. COMPLETE CHECK-IN FRAMEWORKS**

### **A. Evening Check-in v19 (18 Questions | 4-5 minutes)**

#### **SECTION 1: VITAL RHYTHMS** (5 questions - ~90 seconds)
- **Q1:** Energy Pattern Assessment (steady, morning peak, crashes, etc.)
- **Q2:** Energy Timing Analysis (time blocks + context)
- **Q3:** Tiredness Assessment (normal daily depletion, 0-10 scale)
- **Q4:** Fatigue Assessment (pathological exhaustion, medical distinction)
- **Q5:** Sleep Readiness Assessment (0-10 with barriers identification)

#### **SECTION 2: SLEEP ENVIRONMENT & SUBSTANCES** (2 questions - ~30 seconds)
- **Q6:** Sleep Environment Quality (0-10 with environmental factors)
- **Q7:** Caffeine & Alcohol Consumption (combined timing after 2 PM)

#### **SECTION 3: PHYSICAL SYMPTOMS & BODY SIGNALS** (2 questions - ~30 seconds)
- **Q8:** Pain/Discomfort Assessment (0-10 with body location mapping)
- **Q9:** New/Unusual Symptoms (with smart trigger timing attribution)

#### **SECTION 4: HYDRATION & NUTRITION IMPACT** (1 question - ~15 seconds)
- **Q10:** Water Intake Assessment (combined quantity + effects)

#### **SECTION 5: MENTAL & EMOTIONAL STATE** (2 questions - ~30 seconds)
- **Q11:** Daily Stress Level (0-10 with stress source identification)
- **Q12:** Evening Anxiety/Worry (0-10 with anxiety source mapping)

#### **SECTION 6: ACTIVITY & REFLECTION** (2 questions - ~30 seconds)
- **Q13:** Physical Activity Assessment (activity + effects + timing)
- **Q14:** Positive Reflection (multi-select with mood impact rating)

#### **SECTION 7: CORRELATION BRIDGE QUESTIONS** (4 questions - ~60 seconds)
- **Q15:** Food-Energy Bridge (meal timing effects on energy patterns)
- **Q16:** Stress-Symptoms Bridge (physical manifestations of daily stress)
- **Q17:** Activity-Recovery Bridge (recovery expectations from today's activity)
- **Q18:** Environment-Wellness Bridge (environmental factors affecting wellness)
- **Q19:** Food Journal Completion (photo capture completion check)

#### **Key Features:**
- ✅ Evidence-based tired vs fatigue medical distinction
- ✅ Smart conditional logic minimizing question fatigue
- ✅ Complete correlation loops for all 4 identified gaps
- ✅ Medical accuracy with lifestyle optimization focus
- ✅ Integration with photo journal system

### **B. Morning Check-in Framework (24 Questions | 4-5 minutes)**

#### **SECTION 1: VITAL RHYTHMS** (8 questions)
- Sleep duration, quality, continuity, timing, wake method, restoration state, energy level, dominant mood

#### **SECTION 2: BODY SIGNALS** (4 questions)  
- Pain assessment, symptom presence, physical energy, energy-body connection

#### **SECTION 3: MOOD & MINDSET** (3 questions)
- Mental clarity, stress anticipation, gratitude practice

#### **SECTION 4: FUEL & CARE** (3 questions)
- Hunger/appetite, hydration needs, supplement/medication adherence

#### **SECTION 5: ENVIRONMENTAL & LIFESTYLE CONTEXT** (6 questions)
- Yesterday's alcohol consumption, caffeine after 2pm, physical activity, stress events, environmental perception, notable observations

#### **VISUAL BASELINE DOCUMENTATION** (4 photos - 30 seconds)
- Face health assessment, tongue analysis, body profile, targeted health concerns

## **2. PHOTO JOURNAL SYSTEM**

### **A. Comprehensive Capture Strategy**
- **🍽️ Meals:** Breakfast, lunch, dinner with complete AI recognition
- **🍎 Snacks & Desserts:** Between-meal consumption with portion tracking
- **🥤 Beverages:** All non-water drinks (alcohol, caffeine, smoothies)
- **💊 Supplements:** Morning/evening with dosage verification
- **💉 Medications:** Prescription timing confirmation

### **B. Three-Tier AI Recognition System**

#### **🟢 Tier 1: High-Confidence Database Match (85%+ Confidence)**
- USDA whole foods with clear identification
- Packaged items with barcode/label recognition
- Auto-population with minimal user confirmation

#### **🟡 Tier 2: Strategic Database Matching (60-85% Confidence)**
- Common foods requiring refinement for exact database match
- 1-2 strategic questions for nutritional accuracy
- Chain restaurant items with official menu data

#### **🔴 Tier 3: Estimation Mode (Restaurant/Complex Foods)**
- Independent restaurant meals with no database match
- Complex homemade dishes requiring component analysis
- Honest calorie ranges with confidence indicators

### **C. Database Integration Strategy**

#### **Phase 1: Free Database Foundation**
- **USDA FoodData Central:** 300,000+ government-verified items (primary)
- **Open Food Facts:** 2.8M+ products for barcode scanning (secondary)
- **Target:** 75% accurate database matches, $0 monthly cost

#### **Phase 2: Licensed Enhancement**
- **Nutritionix API:** 764,000+ items including restaurant chains
- **Cost:** $50-200/month when 1,000+ active users
- **Target:** 90% accurate database matches

### **D. User Experience Strategy**
- **Week 1:** Zero questions, accept 75% accuracy, build photo habit
- **Week 2:** One strategic question max, improve to 85% accuracy
- **Week 3+:** AI learning reduces effort while improving accuracy
- **Always optional:** Users can accept estimates or refine for precision

## **5. TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE**

### **A. Photo Processing Pipeline**
1. **Photo capture:** Floating action button (always accessible)
2. **AI food identification:** 2-3 seconds processing time
3. **Database search:** USDA → Open Food Facts → Nutritionix priority system
4. **User refinement:** Strategic questions for exact database matching
5. **Nutrition storage:** Final data with confidence metrics and source attribution
6. **Optional photo deletion:** Nutrition data retained, photos deletable for privacy

### **B. Performance Targets**
- **Photo to database lookup:** <5 seconds
- **Full processing with user input:** <30 seconds
- **Offline capability:** Local cache of common USDA items
- **Batch processing:** Handle 10+ photos efficiently
- **API management:** Rate limiting and caching for cost optimization

### **C. Data Storage Strategy**
- **Photos:** Local device storage (encrypted) with optional cloud backup
- **Nutrition data:** Standardized format with confidence scores
- **Check-in data:** Complete correlation dataset with temporal indexing
- **Privacy:** Face recognition disabled, local processing priority
