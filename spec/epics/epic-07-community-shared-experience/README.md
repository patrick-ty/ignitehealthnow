# Epic 7 — Community & Shared Experience

## Goal
Enable safe, pseudonymous community interactions (posts, threads) with private messaging.

## Included (v1 scope)
- Posts
- Threads/comments
- Likes/reactions (optional)
- Messaging

## Excluded
- Public sharing of PHI
- Real-name requirements

## Notes
Privacy controls and moderation requirements are critical and must align with Epic 8.
