# Epic 2 — Intake & Output Tracking

## Goal
Capture what goes **into** and **out of** the body in a structured, time-anchored way to support pattern discovery and correlations.

## Included (v1 scope)
- Food intake quick log
- Drink intake quick log
- Medications quick log
- Supplements quick log
- Output tracking (poop/vomit) quick log

## Excluded
- Nutrition database integration (future)
- Barcode scanning (future)
- Medical advice/diagnosis (never)

## Notes
All logs should be fast, one-screen flows where possible and always time-anchored.
